# Install Lekhai App on Mac

## Step 1

Download Lekhai App from:

[(https://lekhai.app)]

## Step 2

Open the downloaded installer.

## Step 3

Move Lekhai App to the Applications folder.

## Step 4

Launch Lekhai App.

## Step 5

Complete the initial setup.

## Step 6

Open the AI Models section to access supported local AI models.

# Run Llama 4 Locally

## Step 1

Open Lekhai App.

## Step 2

Navigate to the Models section.

## Step 3

Search for:

- Llama 4 Scout
- Llama 4 Maverick

## Step 4

Download your preferred model.

## Step 5

Create a new chat.

## Step 6

Select Llama 4 as the active model.

## Step 7

Start chatting locally on your Mac.
