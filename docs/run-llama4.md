# Run Llama 4 Locally 
## Step 1 

Open Lekhai App.

## Step 2 

Navigate to the Models section. 

## Step 3 

Search for: - Llama 4 Scout - Llama 4 Maverick 

## Step 4 

Download your preferred model. 

## Step 5 

Create a new chat. 

## Step 6 

Select Llama 4 as the active model. 

## Step 7 

Start chatting locally on your Mac.
